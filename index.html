❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app calculate my Daily Goals?

The system uses medical guidelines based on your inputted age to calculate optimal step targets, sleep duration, and water intake to keep your body functioning at its best.

What happens if I miss a day of logging?

Your current streak (the fire icon) will reset to 0. However, don't worry! All your historical data, custom foods, and unlocked trophies are permanently saved in the cloud.

How is my data stored?

Healthy You uses Firebase Cloud Firestore. Your data is securely attached to your email account, allowing you to log in from your phone or laptop and see the exact same metrics.
